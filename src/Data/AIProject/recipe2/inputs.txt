Mexican Black Beans
Ingredients:
2 tbsp oil
1 tbsp dried onion
0.25 tsp dried minced garlic (or 2 cloves)
1 bay leaf
2 tbsp taco seasoning
1 lb black beans
5 cups water
Directions:
1. Add beans to a pot. Cover with water. Bring to a roaring boil and turn heat off. Let sit for 1 hour.  1.
2. Drain beans. Leave beans in the strainer.
3. Add oil to empty pot. Add the onions and garlic to the pot, and saute for about 3 minutes.
4.  Add the bay leaf and spices.
5.  Add the black beans and water, stirring to deglaze and incorporate the flavorful veggie bits from the bottom of the pot into the beans.
6.  Bring water to a simmer. Cover pot and let simmer for 60 - 90 minutes.
7.  When beans are soft and cooked, turn off heat.
8.  Add lime juice. Let cool.
Portion out into zip lock bags for freezing. Remove air from ziplock. Make sure in add some liquid in each bag to protect beans and add flavor.
If cooking from frozen, let it thaw in fridge for 24 - 72 hours first. Or let sit in water bath for 3 hours. Warm over low heat on stove top.
