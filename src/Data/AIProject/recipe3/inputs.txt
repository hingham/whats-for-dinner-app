White Bean Chili
Ingredients:

1 small yellow onion , diced
1 tbsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic , finely minced
3 whole carrots , peeled and chopped
2 (14.5 oz) cans low-sodium chicken broth
1 (7 oz) can diced green chilies
1 1/2 tsp cumin
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp ground coriander
1 tsp cayenne pepper
0 and freshly ground black pepper , to taste
1 (8 oz) pkg Neufchatel cheese (aka light cream cheese), cut into small cubes
1 1/4 cup frozen or fresh corn
2 can 15 oz cans cannellini beans
2 1/2 cups shredded cooked chicken
1 Tbsp fresh lime juice
2 Tbsp masa harina
2 Tbsp chopped fresh cilantro, plus more for serving
0 Tortilla chips or strips, monterrey jack cheese, sliced avocado, or green onion for serving (optional)
0
Directions:
1.  Saute onions first. Then add garlic and carrots.
2.  Add broth, spices, and chilies.
3.  Cook on high 3 hours or low 5 - 6 hours (or until chicken is cooked through)
4.  Make pureed bean mixture with one cup beans, softened light cream cheese (or cottage cheese), and a bit of broth.
5.  Measure out one cup of broth and mix in the masa harina to combine. Add back into broth.
6.  Stir in pureed bean mixture, remaining beans, corn, shredded chicken, lime and cilantro. Heat through. (reserve corn if planning to freeze batch as refreezing twice can effect taste)
Freeze it in ziplock bags for longer-term storage.
If cooking from frozen, let it thaw in fridge for 24 - 72 hours first. Or let sit in water bath for 3 hours.
